Daily Briefing

Your one-stop shop for weather, traffic, news, events, and pictures going on in your city of choice. As students of Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in Piscataway, NJ, we created the Daily Briefing as a front-end project utilizing many web tools including HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, MaterializeCSS, and AJAX. API's from The Dark Sky Forecast, Google Maps, Bing (for news), EventBrite, and Flickr are also used. Firebase is used for the 'Like' button counter.
